Chandler James

Actor / Playwright / Screenwriter

Chandler is an actor and a writer based between Edinburgh and London. He is originally from the American Midwest. He holds his BA in Theatre and English from Cornell University (2011-2015), his MA in Acting from the Guildhall School of Music & Drama (2017-2020), and his MLitt in Playwriting and Screenwriting from the University of St Andrews (2020-2021). In his spare time, Chandler is an avid swimmer, cyclist, and hiker.
